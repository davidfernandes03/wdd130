Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website will be to promote the sport, aiming to reach a wider audience. We also have as objective to inform about the functionality, safety, equipment, and preparation for the sport, in order to generate more confidence in those who wish to experience rafting.

Audience

Target Audience Statement: Our target audience consists of young adults, families, and groups of friends who enjoy spending time outdoors and living experiences worth sharing with others.

What do they want?
Our target customers seek an exciting rafting experience that combines adrenaline, connection with nature, and group fun. They are looking for something more, to create lasting memories and enjoy moments of adventure with friends and family.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accesing the website?
When accessing the website, our target customers expect to find detailed information about the different rafting tours offered, including difficulty levels, safety requirements, necessary equipment, and prices. A comprehensive guide before embarking on the great rafting journey.

What are their needs that aren't being met?
The lack of clear and detailed information about available rafting tours, the absence of convenient online booking options, and the need for testimonials and reviews from previous customers to help them make decisions about their rafting experiences.
